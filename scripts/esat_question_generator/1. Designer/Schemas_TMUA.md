## **M1. Relate Sum to Individual Terms**

**Core thinking move**
Relate the sum of a series to its individual terms to find a relationship between variables.

**Seen in / context**
- Recognising a series within an equation.
- Using the sum of the series to simplify the equation.
- Relating the simplified equation to individual terms.

**Possible wrong paths**
- Incorrectly calculating the sum of the series.
- Failing to recognise the series within the equation.
- Not relating the sum back to the individual terms.

**Notes for generation**
- Consider series such as arithmetic or geometric progressions.
- The relationship might involve equating coefficients.
- From TMUA Paper 1 (Mathematical Knowledge)
