Correct Answer: C

SOLUTION
Reasoning:

Let the linear scale factor from solid$P$to solid$Q$be$k$. For geometrically similar objects, the ratio of their surface areas is equal to the square of the linear scale factor:$$\frac{\text{Surface Area}_Q}{\text{Surface Area}_P} = k^2$$Given that the surface area of$Q$is$4$times that of$P$:$$k^2 = 4 \implies k = 2$$The ratio of their volumes is equal to the cube of the linear scale factor:$$\frac{\text{Volume}_Q}{\text{Volume}_P} = k^3$$Substituting$k=2$:$$\text{Volume}_Q = 2^3 \times V = 8V$$Hence the correct answer is$8V$.
